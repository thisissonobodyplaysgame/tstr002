# TST R002 Electric Bike

The **TST R002** is a high-performance moped-style electric bike designed for speed, comfort, and versatility. Equipped with a **1500W peak rear hub motor**, a **48V removable lithium battery**, and a top speed of up to **32 MPH unlocked**, it delivers strong acceleration and dependable range for both urban and off-road riding.

## Features

* 1500W Peak Rear Hub Motor
* 48V Removable Lithium Battery
* Up to 32 MPH (Unlocked)
* 20” x 4.0” Fat Tires
* Full Front and Rear Suspension
* Hydraulic Disc Brakes
* Shimano 7-Speed Drivetrain
* LED Headlight and Taillight
* LCD Display with App Connectivity

## Riding Modes

* Pedal Assist
* Throttle Only
* Pedal Only

## Overview

Built with a durable frame and retro-inspired mini-bike styling, the TST R002 combines performance and practicality. Its full suspension system and fat tires provide a smooth ride across a variety of terrains, while the hydraulic braking system ensures reliable stopping power. This bike is ideal for commuting, recreational rides, and light off-road use.
